Enter the World of Black Sustainability

AbeDua Link exists as a platform for connecting Afro Global People with an interest in sustainability
AbeDua Link is the official networking site of the 2018 Black Sustainability Summit.

Abe Dua is an adinkra, symbolized by the fern. Abe Dua represents the qualities of wealth, resourcefullness, and self-sufficiency. AbeDua World is an online platform for actualizing the properties of the Adinkra through relationship building, training, and project implementation support.

The AbeDua World contains three modules: Link, Learn, and Launch.

The Link Module contains a social media site for like-minded people to find and connect with each other.
The Learn Module is an online University for Sustainable Development.
The Launch Module offers project management reasons for teams that are ready to take action!

The AbeDua World is a private, membership based web platform. Membership fees support the platform infrastructure, provide jobs for members of the community, and fund grants for projects on AbeDua Launch.
